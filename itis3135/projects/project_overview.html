Basketball Interest & Information - Project Overview

1. Purpose of the Website

This website aims to serve as an informative and interactive platform for individuals interested in basketball. It will provide essential information about basketball at different levels, including UNCC intramural teams, college basketball, and the NBA. Additionally, it will highlight the journey and aspirations of the client, Matthew, who aspires to become a basketball coach.

2. Intended Users

  • UNCC students interested in joining intramural basketball teams
  • Basketball fans who want to stay informed about college and NBA basketball
  • Individuals interested in learning about a future basketball coach's journey
  • Anyone looking for resources related to the sport

3. Website Content Overview

The website will consist of five primary pages:

  • Home (Introduction Page) – Provides an overview of the website's purpose and navigation options.
  • Basketball Information Page – Covers college basketball and NBA overviews.
  • Client Profile Page – Showcases Matthew’s background, experiences, and coaching aspirations.
  • Ways to Get Involved – Offers information on UNCC intramural basketball, including team sign-ups and meeting schedules.
  • Basketball Trivia Page – Features an interactive basketball trivia quiz to engage users and test their basketball knowledge.
